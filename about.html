Our Story

Small pieces, made to matter

Rayan Store started with a simple idea — that the smallest gifts often mean the most.

Who we are

Built around little keepsakes, not big catalogues

Rayan Store is a Bangladesh-based shop that curates a small, tightly chosen range of keychains, brooches and pocket-sized keepsakes. We don't chase huge catalogues — instead, every product we carry is picked, tested and finished with the kind of attention you'd give a gift for someone you actually care about.

From our first bKash confirmation message to the moment your parcel reaches your door, we treat every order the same way: personally, and with care.

What we stand for
